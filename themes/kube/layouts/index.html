Our Mission

SiteShakeup was started to make web redesign more approachable for local businesses. We believe websites are the ultimate form of advertising and, with the world becoming increasingly connected, it only makes sense for your site to be plugged into the larger community. Local businesses are an integral part of a beautiful ecosystem and we want to ensure you’re serving all the customers you can.

How We Do It

We start with a quick talkdown of your business’s website. We want to know how you feel about it and what you are looking to gain from a revamping service. We’ll jot down some notes and begin drafting a proposal.

The proposal will detail everything we think your site could benefit from, based on the talkdown we did a day earlier. We’ll get this proposal back to you within one business day of our first meeting.

After you’ve had a chance to review the proposal, we’ll get together to discuss your site’s shakeup and how we will bring it to life.
